How to Find Us 如何前往

Toronto City Hall is right in the heart of downtown — easy to reach by TTC or on foot from many directions! 多倫多市政廳位於市中心，乘搭TTC即可輕鬆到達

🚇
By Subway乘搭地鐵 Take Line 1 (Yonge–University) to Queen StationExit at the Queen St W / Bay St exit and walk west along Queen St — City Hall is right there, about a 2-minute walk. 乘搭1號線（Yonge–University）至 Queen站從Queen St W / Bay St出口離站，沿Queen St向西步行約2分鐘即可抵達。
